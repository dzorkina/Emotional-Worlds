About us

My name is Daria. I’m a graphic designer and computer science graduate student specializing in front-end development, machine learning, and AI-driven design. Passionate about creating interactive, emotionally intelligent digital experiences that connect technology and art.

Emotional Worlds invites users to express how they feel and watch their emotions come to life. Using AI-driven sentiment analysis, each message transforms into an immersive world of sound and motion, turning data into emotion and emotion into experience.

How it was created

Emotional Worlds was built as an intersection of graphic design, frontend development,machine learning model and artificial intelligence. The goal was to create an interactive environment that translates user emotions into immersive audiovisual experiences. Every stage of the development was designed to combine aesthetic expression with data-driven logic.

The frontend was developed with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to ensure smooth interaction and responsive design. The website captures user input, sends it to the backend, and dynamically displays the generated emotional scene. All visuals and soundscapes were curated to reflect different emotional states — such as calmness, sadness, excitement, or inspiration.

The backend is powered by Python and integrates a fine-tuned machine learning model for emotion recognition, based on j-hartmann/emotion-english-distilroberta-base (Hugging Face). The model was further trained and customized on an extended dataset to better capture nuanced emotional expressions and adapt to the project’s creative context. The AI processes the user’s text, identifies the underlying emotion, and returns a structured JSON response containing emotion labels, confidence scores, and descriptive prompts. A custom mapping then connects each detected emotion to specific video and audio environments, creating a cohesive emotional experience.

The project demonstrates how AI-driven sentiment analysis can enhance digital experiences, making them more personal and emotionally intelligent. It also explores the role of visual and sound design in shaping human perception, offering a foundation for future research in interactive and therapeutic design.
